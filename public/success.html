Getting Started:

Hi there! I'm Callie the Calendar Corgi, a slack bot for keeping track of fun events, deadlines, and goals for you and your team. I'm also a very good dog!

Get started and create your first countdown by saying
"@callie start date: YYYY-MM-DD, event: eventname", and ask me about countdowns you create by saying
"@callie countdown: eventname".

For example, you could say: @callie start date: 2034-04-05, event: trip to mars

Later, you could say: @callie countdown: trip to mars - then you'd get the countdown for your event.

Ask for "@callie help" to see other instructions, otherwise, I'll be hangin' around, just doing, you know, impawtent dog stuff.
